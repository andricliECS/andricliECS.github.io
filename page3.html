What is an iPhone

The iPhone is a line of smartphones made by Apple. They run Apple's iOS mobile operating system. The first generation iPhone was released on June 29, 2007. Apple has released eleven generations of iPhone models, each accompanied by one of the eleven major releases of the iOS operating system. The original iPhone was described as "revolutionary" and a "game-changer" for the mobile phone industry because you could go on the internet,call, send messages, and use apps and play games.
