What is a MacBook

The MacBook is a laptop, which is a portable computer. It comes from the line of Macintosh. The current ineup of MacBooks consists of 3 types of MacBooks. The normal MacBook, MacBook Pro, and MacBook Air.

MacBook

The MacBook is the regular type of the Macbook series. It was sold from May 2006 to February 2012, and relaunched in 2015. The MacBook is on the low end of the MacBook family as it is not as ultra-portable as the the MacBook Air and not as powerful as the MacBook Pro. The MacBook was aimed at the consumer and education markets.

MacBook Air

The MacBook Air is the very light laptop of the MacBook series. It is the cheapest of all of the MacBooks and it is very portable, initially promoted as the world's thinnest notebook. On the performance side however, it does not do as well as the MacBook Pro.

MacBook Pro

The MacBook Pro is a line of Macintosh portable computers introduced in January 2006. It is the high-end model of the MacBook family and is currently available in 13- and 15-inch screen sizes. A 17-inch version was available between April 2006 and June 2012.
